About Kwara State

Kwara is a state in Western Nigeria, bordered to the east by Kogi State, to the north by Niger State, and to the south by Ekiti, Osun, and Oyo states, while its western border makes up part of the international border with Benin. Its capital is the city of Ilorin and the state has 16 local government areas.

Top Activities to do in Kwara state

Visit Owu fall

Towering about 120 metres above sea level to the base where it cascades to an ice-cold pool, Owu falls is considered as the highest and most spectacular waterfall in the whole of Sub-Saharan Africa. The fall is surrounded by a stretch of mountains extending to Ekiti and Kogi state.
